We Are Fashion

Fashion includes everyone: people of all shapes, ethnicities, race, age, sexual orientation, gender identification, etc. Representation and inclusivity matter. Fashion catalogs, advertisements, social media, clothes, events, and the entire fashion industry must be an accurate representation of the world. Let’s ensure we have a fashion industry where no one is left out.
